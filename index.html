Our
Approach

Advancement is achieved through purposeful planning and hard work.

Opportunity transforms into advancement when:

  • Your mission is relevant and meaningful
  • Your work is valued by others
  • Your organization and its leaders are trustworthy
  • Your organization and its leaders keep their promises to the community

Active Listening

Every client, every situation and every community is unique. Our first objective is to understand the specific needs of your organization within the context of your community.

Custom Solutions

Jeanne Marie Ellis

Founder & Principal

A boutique firm in Austin specializing in advancement strategies for nonprofit organizations. Customized solutions for strategic planning, resource development, and community relations.

Jeanne Marie holds a bachelor’s degree in Public Relations from Texas Tech University. Her career spans over three decades and includes strategic planning, nonprofit operations, public relations, marketing, financial development, volunteer development and facility development.

She co-authored a national study for the YMCA called Doing More Together, which explores the rising trends among municipalities partnering with YMCAs to address specific community needs.

Jeanne Marie and her husband, Tom, have two grown children. They attend Austin Christian Fellowship, where she serves on the Missions Leadership Team.
